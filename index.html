This is Butter Chicken

If you're unfamiliar with butter chicken, also known as murgh makhani, it's a popular Indian dish. It was originally created by Kundan Lal Gujral, a chef at Moti Mahal restaurant in Delhi, in 1947. This amazing dish is now well known all around the world definetly a top 5 dish. Here are my 3 recommendations butter chichen spots here in tucson arizona. ~Sher-e-Punjab~ this restaurant is located on 853 E Grant Rd, Tucson, AZ 85719. Number 2, ~Indian Twist~ this is located on 4660 E Camp Lowell Dr, Tucson, AZ 85712. Last and Final spot is ~Spice Box~ this is on 238 N 4th Ave, Tucson, AZ 85705. Those Are my recommendations. If you want to make it at home here is the recepie

1.Marinate The Chicken and refrigerate.

2.Make the punjabi garam masala powder or decide on a good garam masala

3.puree the tomatoes and cashews in a blender until smooth. Filter through a colander to remove the skin and seeds.

4.Soak the almonds or cashews for 1 to 2 hours and use.

5.The color of your gravy may differ based on your red chili powder. you may want to arrange for a good chili powder that is less hot like kashmiri chilli powder and salt.

6.Grill the chicken in the oven or stove top. For grilling in oven,preheat the oven to 240 C for at least 15 min. Skew the chicken on to soaked wooden skewers or place on a greased tray and grill for 20 to 30 min. Flip the chicken to the undone side once one is done.
